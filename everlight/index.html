Electronic Media Studio: Interactivity

This is an excersize in creating a small-scale, Arduino-powered animatronic. For this swinging monkey, I used a cereal box, three plastic knives, leaves from a tree and bush, a fridge-magnet monkey and an Arduino Uno with a servo motor. The only issue with the final product was that the monkey's magnetic hands tended to stick together rather than staying apart in a freeform, "swinging in the jungle" kidn of way.

You can view the original project documentation on the class website.
