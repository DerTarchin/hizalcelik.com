Between the Lines

An abstract, experimental sci-fi art video.

category

Art, Video

date

Fall 2017

technologies used

After Effects, Premiere Pro, Twixtor Plugin

course

Experimental Science Fiction

Mixed Media, Found Footage

The footage used in this video is made up of a variety of sources, including found footage, clips from movies and music videos, and video clips I shot using a macro lens in our studio.

Part I

The first part of this video focuses on abstract and unknown shapes, whisps, fluids and more, set to an eerie, growing soundtrack. The scenes are reminiscint of art films, 2001: A Space Odyssey, and other abstract sci-fi scenes. The part is almost entirely a reddish tone, giving a sense of danger. The soundtrack was inspired by the various "super slow motion" songs, where a song is slowed down so much that it becomes this eerie, ambient track.

Part I

Part II

The second part of this video remixes modified footage from films like Samsara, Baraka and Koyaanisqatsi, and ends with scenes from the music video Horizons by Bassnectar. It is a more driving, awe-inspring segment showing parallel worlds and dizzying effects.
