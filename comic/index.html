Ordinary Comic

A comic about literally thinking outside the box.

category

Art

date

Fall 2015

course

Concept Studio: Self and Human Being

The comic is a basic play on the idea of thinking outside of the box, of characters who become self-aware of the world they exist in, and a humorous demise for those who choose to explore. This is also an experiment in flow of dialogue in a non-linear comic, and interaction between 2D and 3D art pieces.
