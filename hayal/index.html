Hayâl

Building a world.

category

Art, Video

date

Fall 2017

technologies used

Photoshop, Premiere Pro

course

Experimental Science Fiction

Music by Arcade Fire

This short film is a visual representation of one of my daydreaming experiences are like, such as when I'm sitting on the toilet or stuck in a waiting room. Especially when I'm listening to instrumental music, my mind tends to wander and can create unique worlds and scenarios such as this. In Türkish, Hayâl means "dream."

This artwork depicts scenes of what looks like an abandoned civilization, perhaps in the lonely depths of an aquatic world, yellowed and forgotten with age. Scenes of frayed ropes, crumbling buildings, and murky waters potentially instill feelings of curiousity, cautious wonder, and loneliness in the viewer.

Process

This one-day sci-fi studio assignment was to build a world, and I built it using an assortment of personal photos and videos, as well as a photocollage from some inspiration of dense, futuristic cityscapes.

The footage used was actually from my trip to NYC, where a water-based artwork was displayed somewhere on the High Line in the meat district.
