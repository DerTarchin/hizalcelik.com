Short action film made in 3 hours.

category

Video

date

Spring 2016

technologies used

Premiere Pro, Canon Rebel t4i with 50mm prime and 18-135mm lenses

course

Technical Directing

This project was filmed in within one 3 hour class. Light setup and camerawork was done by myself and a few other students. Scripting, screenplay, directing and editing was done by me. One of the highlights of this experience was teaching the actor how to brake-boost the professor's car that we used for filming. Brake-boosting allows an automatic car to spin its tires during launch.
