Satire Through History

Reimaginging classic video games in a Post-Trump context.

The concept behind this satirical piece is to reimagine the worlds and characters of a number of classic 8-bit video games in the context of the state of America after Trump's election. You can interact with the live piece, in the form of a website, below.

Click here to view in fullscreen.

Mario

One of Trump's policies and beliefs is rolling back environmental protection and climate change measures. One major side effect of climate change is rising sea levels around the globe. This Mario world shows what might happen to coastal cities and homes if Trump continues his policies.

Metroid

Trump is an impulsive, dramatic person, making it a scary possibilty that someone with access to the world's largest nuclear stockpile could use it at any moment. I modified Metroid to make it seem like this is an underground bunker after nuclear fallout from Trump's nuclear warfare.

Kirby

Kirby's airy, cute cloud-filled skies are the background for all of his floaty, bouncy adventures. However, due to Trump's ongoing attempts to roll back environmental protections and dismantle measures fighting climate change, Kirby's post-Trump world looks more smoggy, with polution filling the air.

Pokémon

Pokémon games are all about travelling, exploring and interacting with people and creatures. One of Trump's strongests stances is against immigration, particularly from the southern border with Mexico. In this post-Trump world, Pokémon players are met with a border wall that Trump has built.

Zelda

In the original game, Zelda is fighting these little monsters. Trump and many of his followers have expressed extremely racist and bigotted thoughts and remarks, giving racists a safespace and platform to rise up and be more vocal and prevalent. Therefore, Zelda is fighting members of the KKK in post-Trump America.
