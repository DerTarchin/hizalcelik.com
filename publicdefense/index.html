Public Defense Crisis

Informative website about the indigent defense crisis.

category

Code

date

Fall 2016

technologies used

HTML, CSS, Javascript

course
Digital Storytelling and Resistance

This research project and presentation was created as part of our On Commentary assignment, which focuses on creating a digital medium to tell a story or concept from a different or new perspective. I chose to work on the publc defense crisis in America, which continues to be a growing problem to our social (and economic) standings as a nation, among the many other issues the USA faces culturally and systemically.

This project uses a variety of modern Javascript transition and data visualization techniques and libraries to effectively convey facts and information in an entertaining and eye-opening way.
