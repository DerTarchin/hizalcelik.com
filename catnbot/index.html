Cats in Space with No Fuel

A cat and a robot helper exploring space!

category

Game Design, Illustration, Art

date

Fall 2017

technologies used

Microsoft PowerPoint, Unity

course

Experimental Game Design

Cats in Space with No Fuel is a Unity-powered game coded by Matthew Bofenkamp and designed by myself. The premise is that a spaceship of cats has become stranded after running out of fuel, and one brave cat, with the help of their robotic friend, has to to go out exploring the dangerous depths of space to retrieve fuel.

To play the game, you must direct the cat and bot around space, trying to collect enough floating gold nuggets to afford enough gas for the ship. The player can use the bot, which moves inversely in a circlular motion to the cat, to push away dangerous asteroids. If enough asteroids hit the cat, the poor cat will die. Each time the player has collected enough gold, they must find the randomly located space station to purchase gold, then bring it back to the ship. This process must be repeated a few times.
